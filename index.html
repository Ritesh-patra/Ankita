About Me

The Journey of Excellence

I am Ankita Dash, an actress who predominantly works in regional cinema. I came into the limelight after winning the popular reality show ‚ÄúMitha Mitha Jodi‚Äù on Colors Odia TV. The show was a unique blend of acting and dance, created to discover fresh talent and select the lead pair for the Odia film Mitha Mitha.

With my expressive performances, graceful dance skills, and natural screen presence, I emerged as the winner of the reality show, which gave me the opportunity to make my debut as the female lead in the film Mitha Mitha. Since then, I have appeared as a leading actress in several Odia films, gradually building a strong connection with audiences.

I received widespread appreciation for my powerful female-centric role in the 2025 film Vaastav, which was both critically acclaimed and highly appreciated by viewers. This role further strengthened my journey and helped establish me as one of the promising actresses in the industry.
